### Challenge

Business leads arriving through WhatsApp were manually entered into spreadsheets, causing delays and missed opportunities.

### Solution

### Workflow

WhatsApp → n8n → Google Sheets → Notification

### Results

- Faster lead processing
- Reduced manual data entry
- Improved response times
- Better lead tracking
